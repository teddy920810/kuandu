Strawberry thief Red 红色草莓小偷纯棉四件套

零售价 6680.00 元

The eye catching Strawberry Thief is one of William Morris’ most iconic designs. He was inspired to create the original fabric after watching thrushes stealing strawberries from the kitchen garden of his countryside home in Oxfordshire. This enduringly elegant design has been re-coloured in a striking red colourway for this beautiful bedroom range.
“草莓小偷”是威廉.莫里斯最著名的设计之一。其设计灵感源于莫里斯在乡下小屋居住时，看到画眉鸟们偷偷采摘院子里的草莓，顿时被那鲜活的场景所打动，于是创作了这幅经典的作品。这个经典持久的设计采用了让人惊艳的红色，成就了本款美丽的床品。
